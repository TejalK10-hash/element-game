# Match the Right Material for Extreme Engineered Parts

This project is meant for high school and novice material science enthusiasts. 

Every time I was amazed by the breakthrough technologies, thinking profoundly every time I ended up realising that it was the material that it is made of is the prime reason behind it. 

Having started with space and aviation domain, in this project, one gets to explore elements used in the other domains materials namely, advanced computing & electronics, future transportation, mega renewable enrgy systems, extreme scientific machines and bioengineering.
